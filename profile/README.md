# What is whittow? O que é o whittow? ウィットウとは何ですか？ ¿Qué es el whittow? Qu'est-ce que Whittow? Was ist Whittow? Anyone!
[Whittow](https://sites.google.com/view/whittow) Is an editing collaborative site hosted in Base44 and Google sites focused in editing saturation of video (mp4) and images (jpg, png, jpeg and svg) to -64 for 823.
The site has to versions, that is:
* Public/collaborative feed: Hosted on base44
* Private/individual feed: Hosted on google sites
# How to contribute
Go to topbar, click in editor, upload your media, publish in feed or save in hubbox
# Repository making
Whittow only accept creating guides & adversiting repositories in their github page, no topics that is not these topics, remember, **we not store content and we are functional**
# History
Whittow do their work since august 2025, their idea was think in june 2025 as a saturation editor
# Who version become first?
**The base44 version was created first that the google sites version**.
